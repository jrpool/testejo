Accessibility score comparison

Introduction

The table below compares 99 web pages on accessibility. The page names link to the pages on the web. The scores link to digests that explain in detail how the scores were computed.

The pages were:

  1. Tested by Testaro with procedure tp12
  2. Scored by Testilo with procedure sp12a
  3. Digested by Testilo with procedure dp12a
  4. Compared by Testilo with procedure cp12a

The Testaro procedure performs 808 tests on each page. Of these, 16 tests are custom tests defined by Testaro, and the others belong to packages of tests created by others.

Discussion

Tests and scoring formulae are fallible and subjective. The reported faults merit investigation as potential opportunities for improved accessibility. But some may not actually harm accessibility, and some other accessibility faults are not detectable with these tests. Different reasonable procedures could yield different test results and different scores. Testaro and Testilo can be customized to fit different definitions and weightings of types of accessibility.
